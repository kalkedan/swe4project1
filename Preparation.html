finding your reason to be in computing science major is essential that helps you find your interest areas in the fields. It also enables you to prepare you to take on any challenges while you pursue your degree.

luckily, many materials are out there on the internet to either learn early or to understand the materials presented by the professor. There will be free courses, online tutorials made by YouTubers, pdfs, and books to grasp any information about computer science course materials.

depending on how fast you'd learn, computer science has different class settings to choose from, Online class Online class with fast-paced classes In class, regular classes. It is always great to create connections from people who have experience in the class setting before deciding.

The field requires consistency to where you have to be staring the computer most of your time to get the assigned project done, and that needs your hard-working discipline.
